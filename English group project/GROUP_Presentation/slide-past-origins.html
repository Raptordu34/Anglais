The First Seeds — 19th Century

What if machines could evolve and become more powerful than humans?
Samuel Butler — Erewhon, 1872
  • A society that banned machines out of fear
  • Machine as tool — and potential existential threat
  • AI anxiety predates computers by a century
Key insight
Science fiction didn't wait for real AI to exist.
Writers were already imagining — and fearing — intelligent machines 150 years ago.
