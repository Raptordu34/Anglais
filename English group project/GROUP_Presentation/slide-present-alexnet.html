AlexNet — 2012: Machines Learn to See

15.3% error rate — beating the runner-up by 10.8 points

No one had ever seen a gap that large in a competition.

  • ImageNet — 1.2M labeled images, 1,000 categories
  • Team: Krizhevsky, Sutskever, Geoffrey Hinton (Toronto)
  • First proof that deep neural networks trained on GPUs outperform all hand-crafted methods
  • Machines could now see — almost at human level
This is the moment the modern AI era began.
