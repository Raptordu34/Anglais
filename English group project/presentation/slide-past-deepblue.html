1984–1997: The Road to Deep Learning

  • 1984 — The Terminator: Skynet, an AI that turns against humanity. The dystopian vision takes root.
  • 1990 — Rise of Big Data and the internet fuel machine learning.
  • 1997 — IBM's Deep Blue beats chess champion Kasparov. AI defeats humanity's best for the first time.
  • Late 1990s — Deep learning research begins. Neural networks grow larger and more capable.
The Matrix (1999) explores machine domination. A.I. Artificial Intelligence (2001) asks: can robots feel? Sci-fi was already imagining what came next.

Deep Face, Deep Dream — today's AI can recognise faces and generate art. Ethical questions emerge.
