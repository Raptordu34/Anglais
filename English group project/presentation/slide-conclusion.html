Key Takeaways

  • Intelligence is multidimensional — AI replicates several components, but not yet the whole.
  • Sci-fi has always been a mirror — HAL 9000, Terminator, Blade Runner anticipated debates we're having now.
  • Today's AI is powerful but narrow — the leap to AGI requires solving alignment, consciousness, and free will.
  • The future isn't written yet — our choices today will decide whether it looks more like 2001 or Star Wars.
