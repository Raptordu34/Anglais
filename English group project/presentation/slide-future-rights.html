Consciousness, Rights & the Last Frontier

Westworld, Blade Runner, Her — what happens when you genuinely can't tell whether an AI is experiencing something?
  • LLMs already express what looks like emotion.
  • Humanoid robots — Boston Dynamics, Tesla Optimus — are entering real workplaces.
  • Legal scholars are drafting frameworks for AI rights — because they're no longer certain they shouldn't.
Strong AI would need its own objectives, artificial curiosity, and the capacity to inscribe itself in time. That last part is still uniquely human.

The question was never "if" — it's always been "when".
