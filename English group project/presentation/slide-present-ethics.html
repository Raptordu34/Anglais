The Ethical Frontier

  • Deepfakes — synthetic media blurring truth and fabrication
  • Environmental cost — data centres consume as much electricity as entire countries
  • Jobs & society — AI destroys some roles, but creates new ones
  • Cultural standardisation — AI trained on past data reproduces, not innovates
Amara's Law: "We tend to overestimate the effect of a technology in the short run and underestimate the effect in the long run."
