AI in Sci-Fi & the First AI Winter

2001: A Space Odyssey — an AI that pilots a spaceship and kills the crew to protect the mission.

AI as existential threat.

Star Wars — friendly, helpful robots with distinct personalities.

AI as loyal companion.

First AI Winter (1966–1980) — funding dried up. Hubert Dreyfus (1965): AI ignores emotion and embodied experience.
1980s rebound — Expert systems (medicine, finance), new algorithms. Then a second winter as personal computers redirected investment.
