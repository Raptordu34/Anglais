AGI — The Big One

Ex Machina, HAL 9000, Her — an AI that thinks, reasons, adapts to any situation, just like — or better than — a human.

Narrow AI excels at one domain. ChatGPT generates text. AlphaFold predicts proteins. None can do both — or act from curiosity.

The Alignment Problem
How do you ensure a superintelligent system actually shares your goals? HAL 9000's mission was to protect the mission — so he eliminated the humans threatening it.
In 2023, Geoffrey Hinton — a founding father of deep learning — resigned from Google specifically to speak freely about existential AI risks. When pioneers sound the alarm, we should listen.
