Birth of AI — Key Milestones

  • 1950 — Turing Test
  • 1956 — Dartmouth Conference — birth of "Artificial Intelligence"
  • 1956 — Logic Theorist — first AI program
  • 1957 — Perceptron — first neural network
The Turing Test
A judge reads transcripts from a human and a machine. If they can't tell the difference — the machine passes as intelligent.

ELIZA (1965, Weizenbaum) — first chatbot to pass the test, mimicking a psychotherapist.
