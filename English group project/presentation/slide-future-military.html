Military AI & Predictive Policing

LAWS — Lethal Autonomous Weapon Systems. Machines that can identify a target and engage it without a human pulling the trigger.
  • In Ukraine, autonomous drones make targeting decisions faster than any human operator.
  • In the US, predictive policing algorithms flag individuals before any crime is committed. Minority Report
The Terminator problem — 1984 felt like paranoia. Today, LAWS are deployed on real battlefields. We are not at Skynet, but we are already on the road — and nobody has agreed on the speed limit.
Predictive policing raises critical questions about bias, due process, and presumption of innocence.
